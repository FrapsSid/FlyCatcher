# Fly Catcher - Genetic Algorithm Training

Fly Catcher is an interactive Pygame simulation that demonstrates evolutionary strategies through a genetic algorithm. In this project, teams of digital frogs compete to catch escaping flies within a timed arena, where their behaviors—including tongue range, vision, positioning, and cooperation—are encoded in genomes. Over successive generations, the algorithm selects the most successful frog teams, crossbreeds their traits, and introduces mutations to optimize hunting efficiency
